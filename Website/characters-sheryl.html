Sheryl

Sheryl is a working mother who is tired and stressed. She constantly fighting with her (second) husband, and her son is not speaking to her or anyone because he took a vow of silence. Sheryl's brother staying with her after he was released from hospital, after he attempted suicide. Her father-in-law is also doing heroin. But Sheryl is staying calm and positive and trying to keep the family together.

Played by Toni Collette
