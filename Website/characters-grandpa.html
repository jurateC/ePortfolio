Grandpa Edwin Hoover

Grandpa

Edwin is Richard's father who lives with the family since he was evicted from the retirement home for taking the drug heroin. He is a foul-mouthed man who loves drugs and reading pornography. Even though he seems very argumentative and unpleasant man he loves his granddaughter and coaches her for the Little Miss Sunshine pageant. During the trip he was grumpy as always but gave each family member great advice and before he died, and he managed to connect with his son.

Played by Alan Arkin
