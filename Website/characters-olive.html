Olive

Olive is Sheryl's and Richard's seven-year daughter. She is a very childish, naive, and loving girl. Olive's dream came truth when she won entrance to the Little Miss Sunshine beauty pageant. Olive's whole family, through all the difficulties and unexpected obstacles, drove 800 miles in their yellow Volkswagen van to support her.

Played by Abigail Breslin
