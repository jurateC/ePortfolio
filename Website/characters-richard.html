Richard Hoover

Richard

Richard is a motivational speaker and a life coach. He tries to force his world view on his family, which they find very annoying and frustrating. Richard wrote a book and is trying to earn money because his family financial situation is not good. All the mayhem during the family trip and the news that the book won’t be released makes Richard lose his patience for a while. He does not accept his failure and makes more effort to make his dream come true.

Played by Greg Kinnear
