Frank Ginsberg

Frank

Frank is Sheryl's gay brother who is staying with the family after being release from the hospital after a suicide attempt. Frank is a successful academic and the number one Proust scholar in the world, but his life is not very happy. His heart was broken by one of his graduate students and he lost his job. All he wants is to be dead. During the road trip Frank become happier and realizes the importance of family.

Played by Steve Carell
