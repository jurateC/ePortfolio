Introduction to Digital Media
with Irene McGinn

The first project of Intoduction to Digial Media course was a group project. The conditions of the brief were:
1. To create a video about our topic which was Identity,Representation, and the Media.
2. To compile the information gathered on our individual research as well as the statistics, articles and websites that reinforced our first thoughts on the topic.
3. To distribute a video under the time limit and to educate our target audience by providing exact information on the subject - that in some cases, minorities are misrepresented and viewed by the public with labels and stereotypes in mind.
4. To fully complete the accompanying report.

The second project was an analysis of the video game “Cyberpunk 2077” (henceforth “the game”) in the context of digital media, and with a focus on ethics and it was an individual project.
I had 3 aims for this project:
• Appraise the game as entertainment from the perspective of a newcomer
• Consider the game as a continuation of the Cyberpunk genre
• Analyse the ethics of the production of the game.
