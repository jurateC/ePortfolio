Visual Creativity
with Katherine Nolan

In the Visual Creativity module I learned about visual communication, what is it and why its important. I did experimental drawing with mixed media in my sketchbook, and I found out what Visual Language is, and its elements. I studied the Psychology of Perception and I learned about Gestalt Psychology. Every week I had to do exercises and self-directed studies about the subject of the week. At the end of the semester, I created an illustration project with the theme "Patterns" . I also created an educational illustration for college students.

The images with my sketchbook I submitted in the college and never thought about taking a photograph beforehand! these couple images I found in my phone and they are very bad quality.
