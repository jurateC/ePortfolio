Web Development1
with Nina Lyons

In Web Development 1 course I was introduced to HTML and CSS. I learned how to create pages and how to link them, how to create navigation and add images. I learned why CSS is important and was introduced to basic styling.

For my assignment I had to create a professional website of a film of my choice to show most of the practical skills that I have learned during the first semester.
I chose to create website about my favourite movie "Little Miss Sunshine" by Jonathan Dayton and Valerie Faris.
