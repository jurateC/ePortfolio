Creative Development
with Margaret Kinsella

In the Creative Development course, I had to visualise the poem "Why We Do It" which was created by students of Creative Digital Media and the poet Colm Keegan.

“reaching out to feel connected through lcd screens all these faces made of pixels, glitches, and lags”

I worked with Photoshop to add different effects to these 6 pictures, then I drew the laptop with marker pen and connected both media to reflect the 2 lines from the poem.

“lectures, lessons, assignments, stuck in the house. digitised voices flying from headphones and speakers”

I connected a picture of myself and a drawing to show the struggle of too much of digital information and the loneliness of studying at home.

Jurate Compton
