About me

My name is Jurate Compton .
I am from Lithuania originally, but for the last 17 years I have lived in Ireland. I came to Ireland for 3 months, but I liked it so much that I stayed here, and Trim town has become my home.
I am a 45 year old student, and I am very proud of myself! After finishing Secondary School, I never got the chance to go to college. Not many of my friends or family back home were going to college, so I followed suit and got a job and started family, but I always wanted something more.
Now I am in my second semester in TU Dublin studying Creative Digital Media and could not be happier. Even though in the beginning it was a little bit hard to adapt, I enjoy every minute of my studying. I learned so much this year and have partly fulfilled my dream.
